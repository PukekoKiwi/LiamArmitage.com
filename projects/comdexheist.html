Comdex Heist - Tackling the Full Asset Pipeline

Comdex Heist is a high-effort work-in-progress animated short project, set in a dystopian, cyberpunk future where archaic ideas and futuristic fantasy blend. The narrative follows the journey of an alchemist, Alkali, who's on the run after a heist against a powerful corporation. Hot on his trail is Siv, a high-ranking, ruthless corporate employee with a terrifying armored form and control over disassembly nanobots. The project blends the thrills of a chase scene with the use of creative gadgets and abilities, heavily inspired by anime such as Fullmetal Alchemist and Jojo's Bizarre Adventure.

For progress on this project, feel free to check out my Notion page for it here!

Role

As the sole creator, I'm taking on the roles of Illustrator and 3D Artist, responsible for a range of tasks, from concept sketches and costume design to character sculpting and rigging. My goal is to bring to life the intricate, magical setting, often consulting with peers to ensure my designs effectively embody my envisioned narrative. (While keeping with the rule of cool!)

Process and Tools

The process includes a range of tasks, starting with Z-Brush for initial character sculpts. I then switch to Blender for retopology and rigging. For texturing, Substance Painter has been a great tool!

Challenges and Learning

While the monetary barriers to certain tools were an initial challenge, the broader difficulty was in managing the scope and expectations of the project. Creating a high-quality animated short is an intense task, but it's one that I am excited to take on. I've learned to balance my ambitions with practical constraints, weaving in this work alongside other Tech Art projects. The opportunity to exercise such creative freedom and receive positive feedback from my peers has been an incredible reward.

Future Plans

While I'm currently occupied with a diversity of Technical-Art-focused projects to deepen my skillset, Comdex Heist remains a long-term goal I am committed to. I intend to follow the flowchart I've established and devote time to the project when available.
